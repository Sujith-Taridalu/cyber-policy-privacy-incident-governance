# NIST and Regulatory Map

## NIST SP 800-53

The source material treated NIST SP 800-53 as a control framework that must be interpreted and tailored, not simply copied. That is a strong governance signal because it reflects judgment rather than checkbox thinking.

## Related Regulatory Themes

- FTC accountability for weak privacy and security practices
- CFAA relevance to unauthorized access scenarios
- CIRCIA and incident reporting expectations
- broader privacy and governance obligations around emerging technology

## Portfolio Value

Framing the material this way shows awareness that governance work often sits at the intersection of control frameworks, law, privacy, and operational decision-making.
