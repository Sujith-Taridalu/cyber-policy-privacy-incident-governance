# Colonial Pipeline and Reporting

The source documents used the Colonial Pipeline ransomware incident as a practical lens for thinking about legal accountability, critical infrastructure weakness, and policy implications.

## Important Takeaways

- critical infrastructure incidents can become national-level governance problems quickly
- weak identity and access practices can trigger outsized operational impact
- reporting and legal implications matter alongside containment and recovery
- ransomware is both a technical and policy problem

## Why This Case Belongs Here

Using a real-world case helps ground the repository in recognizable security issues and makes the governance material easier for recruiters to understand.
